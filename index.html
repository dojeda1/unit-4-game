You will be given a random target score at the start of each round.

There are four gems above. By clicking on a gem you will add a specific amount of points to your current score.

You win the game by matching your current score to the target score, you lose the game if your current score goes above the target score.

The value of each gem is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new round starts, the value of each gem changes.
